\setupalign[hz,hanging,lesshyphenation]

The workshop will serve as a forum for reflection on the glories and challenges of building digital collaborations, embedding best digital practice in individual research, maintaining digital infrastructures, and coping with the possibilities and constraints of the digital medium. A series of internal and external speakers will discuss personal experiences as well as international trends. {\bf The workshop is open to anyone with interest in digital research}.
\blank[5mm]

The list of confirmed speakers includes \goto{Shawn Ross}[url(http://bit.ly/ShawnRossPure)] and \goto{Brian Ballsun-Stanton}[url(http://bit.ly/BrianBallsunStantonPure)] from Macquarie University in Sydney, \goto{Pietro Maria Liuzzo}[url(https://www.betamasaheft.uni-hamburg.de/team/projectteam/liuzzo.html)] from Hamburg University, \goto{Tomáš Hampejs}[url(https://dissinet.cz)] and \goto{Adam Mertel}[url(https://dissinet.cz)] from Masaryk University in Brno, \goto{Katrine Frøkjær Baunvig}[url(http://grundtvigcenteret.au.dk/)] from Aarhus University, \goto{Benjemin Grant Purzycki}[url(https://bgpurzycki.wordpress.com)] from Aarhus University and \goto{Marie Louise Tørring}[url(http://www.netlab.dk/research/it-developer-projects/the-hpv-archive/)] from Aarhus University! The afternoon session will showcase the newly created research group \goto{Social Dynamics in the Ancient Mediterranean (SDAM)}[url(https://sdam-au.github.io/sdam-au/)], based at Aarhus University.
